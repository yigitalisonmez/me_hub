Privacy Policy for Kora

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Kora ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal wellbeing and tracking app developed by Yigit Sönmez. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. Kora is designed to be local-first: your personal data stays on your device.

Summary

  • Kora has no user accounts and requires no sign-in.
  • Your personal entries (todos, water intake, mood, routines, gratitude, affirmations, calendar events, timers, settings) are stored only on your device.
  • We do not operate any server that collects, stores, or syncs your data.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with anyone.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is created by you and stored locally on your device using on-device storage (Hive, SharedPreferences, and secure storage):

  • Tasks/todos, water intake, mood entries, routines, gratitude notes, affirmations, breathing/timer sessions, calendar events, and app preferences.

This data never leaves your device through Kora, except as described in the "Third-Party Services" section below. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Permissions We Request and Why

  • Notifications — to deliver reminders you enable (water, routines, todos, weekly insights, calendar events). You control these in the app and your OS settings.
  • Exact alarms — to deliver scheduled reminders at the time you set.
  • Microphone — only used when you actively start a voice command. Audio is processed for speech recognition (see Third-Party Services) and is not stored by Kora.
  • Internet — used only to fetch a daily inspirational quote from a public quotes service. No personal data is sent.

Third-Party Services

Kora connects to external services in two limited cases:

  • Daily quotes (ZenQuotes API — https://zenquotes.io): When the app fetches the daily quote, it makes a standard network request to ZenQuotes. This request contains no personal data about you. Your device's IP address may be visible to that service, as with any web request.
  • Speech recognition (device/Google speech services): The voice command feature uses your device's built-in speech recognition. On Android, this may transmit the audio you speak to Google's speech recognition service to convert it to text, governed by Google's Privacy Policy. This only happens while you are actively using a voice command. Kora itself does not record, store, or transmit your audio.

Children's Privacy

Kora is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security (screen lock, encryption, etc.). Sensitive values use the platform's secure storage.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app. Because we do not hold your data on any server, there is nothing for us to delete on our side.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version.

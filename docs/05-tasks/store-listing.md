### Short description (≤80)

```
Track water, mood, habits & gratitude. Private, local-first wellbeing app.
```

### Full description (≤4000)
```
Kora is your calm, private space for building better daily habits and taking
care of your wellbeing — all in one simple app.

Everything you track stays on your device. No account, no sign-in, no cloud.
Just you and your progress.

WHAT YOU CAN TRACK
• Water intake — set a daily goal and log every glass with gentle reminders
• Mood — check in daily and see how your feelings change over time
• Routines — build morning and evening rituals that actually stick
• To-dos — a clean, focused task list for your day
• Gratitude — write down what you're thankful for and build a positive habit
• Calendar & reminders — never miss the things that matter

FOCUS & CALM
• Breathing exercises — guided sessions with soothing background sounds
• Affirmations — record and play your own positive affirmations
• Focus timer — Pomodoro-style sessions to get things done

GENTLE REMINDERS
Turn on reminders for water, routines, to-dos, and your weekly insight. You're
always in control — choose what you want and when.

VOICE COMMANDS
Use your voice to add and check things quickly, powered by on-device speech.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Kora is local-first. Your data lives on your phone, not on our servers. No ads,
no trackers, no data selling. Ever.

Start small. Stay consistent. Feel better — with Kora.
```

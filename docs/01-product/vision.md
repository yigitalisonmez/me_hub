# Product Vision

Kora is an all-in-one personal hub for daily productivity, wellness, and
reflection.

## Target Experience

The app should feel calm, personal, fast, and useful every day. It should help a
user answer:

- What should I do today?
- How am I doing physically and emotionally?
- What habits or routines am I building?
- What needs attention soon?
- What small action can I take right now?

## Product Pillars

- Daily clarity: tasks, routines, calendar, timers.
- Wellness tracking: water, mood, breathing.
- Reflection: gratitude and affirmations.
- Motivation: challenges, streaks, progress, quotes.
- Convenience: voice commands. Home widget support is deferred until after v1.

## Design Direction

The current UI uses warm colors, glass/neumorphic elements, card depth, and a
friendly dashboard style. Preserve consistency with the existing core widgets and
theme provider before introducing a new visual direction.
